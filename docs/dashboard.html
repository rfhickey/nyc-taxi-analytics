NYC yellow taxi · Q1 2025 · TLC trip records + Open-Meteo

Ten million taxi trips, one winter quarter

Every chart below is built from the dbt marts of the nyc-taxi-analytics warehouse: one fact table of validated trips and four aggregate marts covering demand, weather, zones, and hours. Hover any mark for exact values; every chart has a table view.

Source marts: fct_trips, hourly_demand_patterns, daily_weather_trip_summary, daily_zone_performance from the nyc-taxi-analytics dbt project (DuckDB), covering 2024-12-31 to 2025-04-01. Trip counts per day-of-week and hour aggregate the full quarter. The TLC feed records credit card tips only, so all tip figures cover credit card trips and are stated against the base meter fare. The two near-empty boundary days come from records that spill across monthly source files and carry no weather observation; the pipeline labels them Unknown rather than folding them into a real weather category.
